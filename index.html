The LEGOBatman Movie

The LEGO Batman movie is a very new addition to the LEGO movie collection.
The movie, made out of computer created bricks, is the story about Batman's relationships with the people around him.
It stars many of DC's superheroes and villains, plus a couple other special characters from many different universes.
